# Empathy Canvas: CampusBot

**Design For:** College Students & Parents | **Design By:** Mox Patel
**Date:** April 3, 2026 | **Version:** 1.0

---

## 👤 USER
- **New Admissions:** Prospective students seeking instant guidance on eligibility, application forms, required documents, and fee structures.
- **Current Students:** Enrolled students demanding seamless access to manage academic life—checking exam schedules, results, events, and hostel facilities.
- **Parents:** Guardians requiring transparent, accessible information on fees, placement updates, and overall college support without needing to visit the campus.

---

## 🤝 STAKEHOLDERS
- **Primary:** Students (Prospective & Current), Parents/Guardians.
- **Secondary:** College Administration Staff (Admissions office, Accounts department, Examination Controllers, Hostel Wardens).
- **Tertiary:** College Management/Board, Event Organizers, Corporate Recruiters.

---

## 📝 ACTIVITIES
- Asking college-related queries via the chatbot 24/7 (covering fees, admissions, exams, and hostels).
- Checking admission eligibility criteria and document checklists for various courses.
- Inquiring about transparent fee structures and digital payment options.
- Checking real-time exam timetables, academic results, and calculating CGPA.
- Registering for campus events and technical workshops using QR codes.
- Seeking updates on upcoming placements, internship opportunities, and scholarships.

---

## 🎬 STORY BOARDING

### 🟢 HAPPY (1)
**Instant Midnight Support:** A busy parent wants to check the fee structure for the IT program at 11:30 PM. Instead of waiting until morning to call the administration office, they use CampusBot on the college website. They instantly receive a semester-wise fee breakdown and EMI options. They feel relieved, informed, and impressed by the college's digital accessibility.

### 🟢 HAPPY (2)
**Seamless Event Registration:** A current student is looking for coding workshops on campus. They type "upcoming workshops" into CampusBot. The bot immediately provides a list of events with a direct registration link. The student registers in 30 seconds and receives an automated reminder the day before the event. They feel productive, engaged, and well-connected to campus life.

### 🔴 SAD (1)
**The Infinite Waiting Line:** It's peak admission season. A prospective student needs to know if they are eligible for the Computer Science program based on their recent test scores. They try calling the college admin, but phone lines are busy all day. They travel 2 hours to the college, only to wait in a crowded queue for another hour, just to ask a quick 2-minute question. They feel frustrated, exhausted, and reconsider joining the college at all.

### 🔴 SAD (2)
**Missed Deadlines & Penalties:** A student misses a crucial deadline to submit their examination fee because the notification was buried deep within the college's complex website notice board. They end up being forced to pay a hefty late fee penalty. They feel stressed, unfairly penalized, and angry about the lack of proactive, direct communication from the institution.
